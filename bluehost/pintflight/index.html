The CABREW Pint Flight

The event starts on April 6th with beers made by our club members on tap in all of the Concord tap rooms. Look for the distinctive handmade CABREW tap handles created by Jon Putnam. The event runs all month or until the pint glasses are gone so get your card filled early.

Each winning brewer will be on hand at the brewery where he brewed his beer to help celibate. Go to your favorite location and try a beer from the CABREW tap. Get a Pint Flight card and have it stamped by your server.

Once you have filled your card by visiting all six locations you can redeem your Pint Flight card for a collectible pint glass with the logos of all participating locations. Come to our club meeting to collect your glass. Come for the glass, stay for the home brew.
